Welcome to your Personal Video Game Recommender! There's just three simple steps you need to take before getting your recommendations.

1. Make sure that your Steam inventory setting is public. You can do this through Steam by clicking on your account name at the top right, go to "view my profile," click edit profile, and then set your profile and inventory to public.

2. Now all you have to do is put in your steam id, a 17 digit user id that comes with every steam account (e.g. 76561197960434622). After writing your id, put down the number of recommendations you'd like. If you put a weird number like 0 or a negative, the default will be 20.

3. Press submit and get your recommendations!
